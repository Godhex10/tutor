About The Tutor

Vincent Akinkuotu

Vincent Akinkuotu, M.A. English, LLM (BL), is the Lead Tutor and founder of Sure Success Point Tutorial College (Akoka, Lagos) – a centre he established in 1999 – and the proprietor of Master Point Schools (Ikorodu, Lagos). With over two decades of experience, he has become a household name in English‑language instruction, guiding countless students who now excel as professors, doctors, engineers and professionals in other fields. In addition to his educational leadership, Vincent is a seasoned lawyer and principal partner at Akinkuotu, Akinkuotu & Co., a member of the Nigerian Bar Association and the International Bar Association. His dual expertise in law and English equips him to teach with clarity, rigor and a touch of fun, making online learning both effective and enjoyable.
